About

WE HELP IN MIGRATION FROM COUNTRY TO COUNTRY

We also give counseling advice to people on how
when and how to migrate from place to place.

Migration is a right, as the movement
of people to another predates human civilization.
People migrate for various reasons:
work, better standard of living, economic oppurtunities
etc. However, people can choose to migrate either in a regular or irregular manner.

Regular migration

is migration that occurs in compliance with the
laws of the country of origin, transit and
destination.

Iregular Migration

is the movement of persons that takes place outside the
laws, regulations, or international agreements governing the entry into
or exit from the State of origin, transit
or destination (IOM).
